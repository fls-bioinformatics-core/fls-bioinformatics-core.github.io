About

The Bioinformatics Core Facility (BCF) is part of the Faculty of Medicine, Biology and Health (FBMH) at the University of Manchester (UoM) in the UK.

This page outlines the key software developed and maintained by the core facility as part of its work processing and analysing data for researchers within the faculty.

For all software and other materials made available by the core facility see our Github organisation page at https://github.com/fls-bioinformatics-core
